Booster K1
==========

The `K1 <https://www.booster.tech/booster-k1/>`_ robot from `Booster <https://www.booster.tech>`_ is a 0.95 meter tall humanoid robot with 22 degrees of freedom and a wight of approximately 19.5kg.
It features 6 DoF per leg, 4 per arm and two for the head.

The K1 robot model used in the simulation is based on the official model files from Booster.
As a result, motions developed for the real robot should also work within the simulation and - with a bit of luck - vice versa.

+--------------------+----------------------------------------------+
| Model Properties                                                  |
+====================+==============================================+
| **Name / ID**      | 'K1'                                         |
+--------------------+----------------------------------------------+
| **Type / Anatomy** | Humanoid Robot                               |
+--------------------+----------------------------------------------+
| **DoF**            | 22 |mdash| 6 per leg, 4 per arm, 2 in neck   |
+--------------------+----------------------------------------------+
