Booster T1
==========

The `T1 <https://www.booster.tech/booster-t1/>`_ robot from `Booster <https://www.booster.tech>`_ is a 1.18 meter tall humanoid robot with 23 degrees of freedom and a wight of approximately 30kg.
It features 6 DoF per leg, 4 per arm, two for the head and one for the waist body.

The T1 robot model used in the simulation is based on the official model files from Booster.
As a result, motions developed for the real robot should also work within the simulation and - with a bit of luck - vice versa.

+--------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------+
| Model Properties                                                                  |
+====================+==============================================================+
| **Name / ID**      | 'T1'                                                         |
+--------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Type / Anatomy** | Humanoid Robot                                               |
+--------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------+
| **DoF**            | 23 |mdash| 6 per leg, 4 per arm, 1 in waist, 2 in neck       |
+--------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------+
